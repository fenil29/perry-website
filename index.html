About me

Mr. Perry Koradiya has completed his Diploma in Mechanical Engineering from Noble Group of Institutions and he has developed a CNC Trainer in his project which is 3half cost than the market price. Also he achieved a Gold Medal for that project and got award in Gandhian Young Technological Award. Also he has completed his Bachelors in Mechanical Engineering from Uka Tarsadia University. He worked as a Production Engineer at Sahajanand Technologies Private Limited, Gujarat-India in 3d printing department. He has a vast experience about 3d printing technology and he has developed his own 3d printer machine. Also he has achieved a Best Employee Award in his Company. Sir has received a Gold Medal for his research on Advance Technologies in Machine Tools. He has published more than six research papers in reputed journals. Also he has active profile in Sports. He is known as a Skating Champion of Tapi District and achieved many Medals in Inline as well as Roller Skating at District & State level Competitions. As a part of responsibility in this Covid-19 situation he has developed a Face Shield and Mask Clips and distributed more than thousands to the Hospitals, Nursing Homes, Industries etc. in Canada and India. He has completed Mechanical Technician CNC Program with Distinction from Conestoga College in Canada. Currently Perry is working as a CNC programmer in canada and also he has his own startup business of 3d printing in India and Canada named “PrintzSmith”. Also Perry is working on a Patent for World’s First 3d Printed Electric Bike which will run with only one tire and works on a battery.
